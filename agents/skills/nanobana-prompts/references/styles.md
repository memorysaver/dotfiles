# Style Reference

Keywords and descriptors for each visual style. Use the core keywords plus 2–3 enhancers from the list.

## Photography

**Core:** photorealistic, photograph, photo, editorial photography
**Enhancers:** shot on [camera model], DSLR quality, RAW photo, natural grain, film stock, medium format
**Lighting:** natural light, studio lighting, golden hour, blue hour, flash photography
**Pairs well with:** technical-specs camera settings
**Avoid combining with:** illustration, hand-drawn, cartoon

## Cinematic

**Core:** cinematic, movie still, film grain, anamorphic
**Enhancers:** color grading, letterboxed, cinematic composition, dramatic framing, shallow depth of field
**Lighting:** dramatic lighting, volumetric light, rim lighting, chiaroscuro, backlighting
**Mood words:** epic, atmospheric, moody, intense, brooding
**Pairs well with:** specific film references ("in the style of Blade Runner 2049")

## Anime / Manga

**Core:** anime style, manga style, anime illustration
**Enhancers:** cel-shaded, clean linework, vibrant colors, detailed eyes, expressive
**Sub-styles:** shonen, shoujo, seinen, mecha, slice of life
**Named styles:** Studio Ghibli, Makoto Shinkai, Kyoto Animation, MAPPA
**Avoid combining with:** photorealistic, DSLR, camera settings

## Illustration

**Core:** illustration, digital illustration, digital art, illustrated
**Enhancers:** detailed, polished, professional illustration, editorial illustration
**Sub-styles:** children's book, botanical, fashion illustration, technical illustration, concept art
**Pairs well with:** specific artist style references

## Sketch

**Core:** sketch, pencil sketch, charcoal sketch, line drawing
**Enhancers:** rough, gestural, cross-hatching, detailed linework, quick study
**Media:** graphite, charcoal, ink, conte crayon, ballpoint pen
**Paper:** on white paper, on toned paper, on kraft paper, sketchbook page

## Comic

**Core:** comic book style, comic panel, graphic novel
**Enhancers:** bold outlines, halftone dots, speech bubbles, dynamic pose, action lines
**Sub-styles:** Marvel/DC style, indie comic, European BD, webcomic, manhwa

## 3D Render

**Core:** 3D render, 3D illustration, CGI
**Renderers:** Cinema 4D, Blender, Octane Render, V-Ray, Unreal Engine 5, KeyShot
**Enhancers:** PBR materials, ray-traced, global illumination, subsurface scattering, caustics
**Sub-styles:** product visualization, architectural visualization, character render

## Chibi

**Core:** chibi, chibi style, super-deformed
**Enhancers:** cute, kawaii, oversized head, small body, simplified features, big eyes
**Pairs well with:** anime, illustration, 3D render (for 3D chibi figurines)

## Isometric

**Core:** isometric, isometric view, 45-degree angle, isometric 3D
**Enhancers:** miniature, diorama, tilt-shift, detailed environment, interconnected
**Platforms:** hexagonal platforms, floating islands, grid-based
**Pairs well with:** 3D render, pixel art, illustration

## Pixel Art

**Core:** pixel art, 8-bit, 16-bit, retro pixel
**Enhancers:** limited palette, dithering, sprite, tile-based
**Resolutions:** low-resolution, 32x32, 64x64, 128x128
**Sub-styles:** game boy style, SNES style, modern pixel art
**Avoid combining with:** photorealistic, high resolution, 4K/8K

## Oil Painting

**Core:** oil painting, oil on canvas, painted
**Enhancers:** visible brushstrokes, impasto, glazing, rich texture, classical
**Named styles:** Impressionist, Renaissance, Baroque, Pre-Raphaelite
**Avoid combining with:** clean, sharp, digital, CGI

## Watercolor

**Core:** watercolor, watercolour, watercolor painting
**Enhancers:** wet-on-wet, soft edges, color bleeding, transparent washes, paper texture
**Mood:** delicate, airy, dreamy, soft, organic
**Avoid combining with:** sharp lines, photorealistic, HDR

## Ink / Chinese Ink

**Core:** ink painting, Chinese ink, sumi-e, ink wash
**Enhancers:** brushwork, negative space, flowing, minimal, traditional
**Paper:** on rice paper, on xuan paper, scroll painting
**Pairs well with:** minimalism, traditional, Eastern aesthetic

## Retro / Vintage

**Core:** retro, vintage, nostalgic, old-school
**Decades:** 1950s, 1960s, 1970s, 1980s, 1990s
**Enhancers:** faded colors, grain, vignette, analog, worn texture, halftone
**Sub-styles:** art deco, art nouveau, mid-century modern, synthwave, vaporwave

## Cyberpunk

**Core:** cyberpunk, futuristic, dystopian, neon
**Enhancers:** neon lights, holographic, chrome, augmented, glitch effects, rain-slicked streets
**Colors:** cyan, magenta, electric blue, neon pink, purple
**Atmosphere:** dark, gritty, urban, smoggy, crowded
**Pairs well with:** cinematic, photography, 3D render

## Minimalism

**Core:** minimalist, minimal, clean, simple
**Enhancers:** negative space, flat design, geometric, limited palette, whitespace
**Colors:** monochrome, two-tone, muted, pastel
**Avoid combining with:** detailed, complex, busy, cluttered, ornate

## Ukiyo-e / Japanese Woodblock

**Core:** ukiyo-e, Japanese woodblock print, Edo-period
**Enhancers:** flat color fields, bold outlines, wave patterns, traditional Japanese composition
**Sub-styles:** kabuki portraits, landscape prints, bijin-ga (beauty prints)
**Named references:** Hokusai, Hiroshige, Utamaro
**Pairs well with:** ink, traditional, Eastern aesthetic

## Liquid Glass / Glassmorphism

**Core:** liquid glass, glassmorphism, frosted glass, translucent
**Enhancers:** refraction, transparency, blur-through, iridescent, frosted surface
**Sub-styles:** bento grid, card UI, premium infographic
**Colors:** white, soft gradients, subtle color tints through glass
**Pairs well with:** 3D render, minimalism, modern UI

## Chalkboard

**Core:** chalkboard style, chalk drawing, blackboard
**Enhancers:** chalk texture, hand-lettered, dust particles, smudged edges
**Colors:** white and colored chalk on dark green or black background
**Pairs well with:** sketch, hand-drawn, educational content

## Hand-drawn / Doodle

**Core:** hand-drawn, doodle, whimsical illustration
**Enhancers:** intentionally imperfect, marker/pen style, playful linework, casual
**Media:** felt-tip marker, ballpoint pen, crayon, colored pencil
**Mood:** fun, approachable, informal, creative
**Pairs well with:** sketch, illustration, social media headers
